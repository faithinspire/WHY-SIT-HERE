### Header Section
- Title: "How to Access Your Book"
- Subtitle: "Why Sit Here and Die? - Complete Access Guide"
- Animation: Fade in down (0.8s)

### 4-Step Process
**Step 1: Visit Website** 🌐
- Icon: Globe
- Details: Open browser, visit website, read about book, see pricing
- Animation: Slide up (0.1s delay)

**Step 2: Make Payment** 💳
- Icon: Credit Card
- Details: Choose payment method, transfer ₦6,000, note reference, confirm
- Animation: Slide up (0.3s delay)

**Step 3: Receive Password** 🔑
- Icon: Key
- Details: Send proof to WhatsApp, wait for confirmation, receive password, save safely
- Animation: Slide up (0.5s delay)

**Step 4: Access & Download** ⬇️
- Icon: Download
- Details: Enter password, read online, download PDF, enjoy book
- Animation: Slide up (0.7s delay)

- Online Reader: Read all 18 chapters
- PDF Download: Professional PDF with 14pt font
- Mobile Friendly: Works on all devices
- Secure Access: Password protected with copy protection

### Pricing Section
- Amount: ₦6,000
- Type: One-time payment
- Duration: Lifetime access
- Animation: Scale in (1.5s)

### CTA Section
- Headline: "Ready to Access Your Book?"
- Button: "Go to Website"
- Info Box: Help and support information
- Animation: Fade in (2s)

- Animation: Fade in (2.5s)
