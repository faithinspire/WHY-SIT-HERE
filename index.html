Why Sit Here and Die?

Breaking the trap of stagnation—spiritually, financially, and personally

A life-and-leadership wake-up call from 2 Kings 7

By OLUSHOLA O. PAUL

When life is starving you—of joy, money, progress, peace—there is a question that exposes every excuse: Why sit here and die?

In 2 Kings 7, four rejected lepers made one decision that saved a city: they moved. Their story is not just ancient history; it is a blueprint for spiritual renewal, career direction, academic excellence, leadership growth, and business breakthrough.

This book will help you confront stagnation, take wise risks, build discipline, steward increase, and turn your testimony into impact—without denying reality and without losing faith.

  • Why rejection can become your greatest teacher
  • How to move with faith when you can't see the whole path
  • The difference between recklessness and wise risk-taking
  • Why your "night moves" determine your public success
  • How God intervenes where your hands cannot reach
  • The stewardship test that comes after every breakthrough
  • Why good news must be shared, not hoarded
  • How to build systems that sustain miracles
  • The legacy you leave through those you raise
  • And much more...
