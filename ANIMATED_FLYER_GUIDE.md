## ✨ ANIMATION EFFECTS

### Entrance Animations
- **Header:** Fade in down (0.8s)
- **Steps:** Slide up with staggered delays (0.1s - 0.7s)
- **Features:** Slide up with staggered delays (1.2s - 1.8s)
- **CTA:** Fade in (2s)
- **Footer:** Fade in (2.5s)

### Interactive Animations
- **Step Hover:** Lift up with shadow (translateY -10px)
- **Icons:** Bounce continuously (2s loop)
- **Numbers:** Pulse continuously (2s loop)
- **Arrows:** Slide right continuously (1s loop)
- **Shimmer:** Shimmer effect on step cards (2s loop)

### Special Effects
- **Timeline:** Expand width (1.5s)
- **Price Tag:** Scale in (0.8s at 1.5s)
- **CTA Button:** Pulse (2s loop at 2.5s)
